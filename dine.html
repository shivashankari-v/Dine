Chef's Masterpiece

Awadhi Nalli Nihari (Uttar Pradesh)

A historic recipe from the Nawab royal kitchens of Lucknow. Slow-cooked overnight for 12 hours with majestic whole spices, tender lamb shanks, and finished with a dash of fragrant saffron water. Best paired with our clay-oven baked Khamiri Roti.
